Frequently Asked Questions

A smart water meter is a digital device that automatically measures your water usage and transmits the data in real-time. Unlike traditional meters that require manual readings, smart meters allow for remote monitoring, instant alerts for leaks or tampering, and provide more accurate billing by recording actual consumption down to the minute.

Billing is calculated based on real-time water usage data captured by your smart meter. This ensures you only pay for what you use. Users can log in to their dashboard to view detailed consumption reports, see billing history, and make secure payments through our online portal—making the process simple and transparent.
